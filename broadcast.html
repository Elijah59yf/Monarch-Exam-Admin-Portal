Email Broadcasting Details

  • • Emails are sent asynchronously in the background
  • • Delivery status is tracked and logged for each recipient
  • • Failed deliveries are retried automatically (up to 3 times)
  • • Email templates can be customized in the backend
  • • All broadcasts are logged for compliance and audit purposes

ℹ️ Broadcast Limitations

Large courses may take several minutes to process. Email delivery depends on the configured email provider (SMTP, SendGrid, etc.). Check the logs for individual delivery status.
